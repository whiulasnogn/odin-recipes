Empress and Tonic

A classic cocktail with a colourful twist.

Ingredients

  • Fancy Cocktail Cup
  • Ice
  • 60ml Empress 1908 Gin
  • 90ml Fever Tree premium Indian tonic water
  • Grapefruit

Steps

  1. Fill the cup 3/4 with ice
  2. Add Empress 1908 Gin
  3. Add tonic water
  4. Add slice of grapefruit for garnish

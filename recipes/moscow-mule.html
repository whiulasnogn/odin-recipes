Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • Copper Cup
  • Ice
  • 1.5 Shot of Vodka
  • 1/2 Medium Lime Juice
  • Ginger Beer

Steps

  1. Fill Copper cup to the top with ice
  2. Add 1.5 shot of Vodka (roughly 60ml)
  3. Squeeze the juice of lime into the cup
  4. Fill cup with your favourite ginger beer (I like Fever Tree)
  5. Garnish with a lime wedge and sprig of mint
